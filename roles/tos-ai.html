TOS-AI — Transition Office Steward (AI pivots)

A temporary executive steward who guides strategy, governance, and operating-model shifts during an organization’s AI transition.

Full definition: docs/defs/TOS-AI.md

When to install a TOS-AI

  • AI strategy exists but governance/risk/assurance are unclear.
  • Many workstreams need one accountable steward for the transition.
  • Org design, policies, or vendors will change within 1–2 quarters.
  • There’s a defined “exit criteria” back to steady-state ownership.
