Roles · How to use this site

Three roles. One shared civic lab.

CoPolitic is an experiment in power-aware, AI-literate governance. Most visitors show up wearing one of three hats. This page helps you pick your lane, then plug into the rest of the site and the CoCivium labs without guessing.

Stewards

Stewards & operators

Builders, public servants, organizers, technologists and researchers who want to make new guardrails real.

  • Start with Why now to lock context and language.
  • Use For stewards as a working brief you can annotate with your team and AI tools.
  • Book a Perspective Plane Lab or Guardrail Jam to stress‑test your org or policy idea.
Your main hallway: For stewards · When ready to run experiments: Labs
Elites

Elites & power‑holders

Investors, executives, funders, public figures and institutional leaders whose decisions steer whole systems.

  • Scan Home and Why now for the high‑level thesis in under five minutes.
  • Use For elites for board decks, LP updates and public positioning.
  • Point your staff to a Civic Twin Sprint lab if you want a low‑risk, high‑signal pilot.
Your main hallway: For elites · Hand this link to your chief of staff: Labs
Youth

Youth & emerging voices

Students, early‑career builders, organizers and everyday citizens who want a say in how AI‑powered institutions evolve.

  • Begin with For youth for plain‑language context and prompts.
  • Use the copy blocks there as input for your own projects, clubs or campaigns.
  • Apply with a mentor or educator to join a lab cohort, or propose a new lab format.
Your main hallway: For youth · When you are ready to co‑run something: Labs
