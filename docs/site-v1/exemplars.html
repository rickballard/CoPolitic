Exemplars and early adopters

CoPolitic exists to help real people in real institutions run safer experiments with power. This page highlights the kinds of partners and pilots we are looking for. It is intentionally light on names. We care more about patterns than trophies.

Who belongs on this page

  • Leaders who are willing to be transparent about how they use artificial intelligence in decisions.
  • Teams that want to test new guardrails in public rather than in a closed lab.
  • Communities that want a voice in how data and algorithms shape their shared future.

Example archetypes

These are sketches. They are here to help you see yourself in the work. If one of these archetypes sounds like you, you can be an early exemplar.

Archetype Where Why it matters Status
City stewardship lab Mayor plus council plus community delegates Tests open guardrails for AI use in local services, budgeting and planning. Seeking first partner
Institutional investor pilot Investment committee plus stewardship council Explores how to align capital flows with long term civic resilience. In design stage
University ethics studio Students plus faculty plus external stewards Lets the next generation practice real governance on safe, bounded problems. Seeking collaborators
Media trust lab Newsroom plus reader panel Experiment with visible guardrails on AI assisted reporting and curation. Concept stage

Could you be an exemplar

If you lead a team, institution or network that should be among the first wave, we want to hear from you. You do not need to be perfect. You do need to care about honesty, accountability and shared benefit.

To explore fit, reach out by email at contact@InSeed.com with a short note about who you are, who you serve and what you are willing to put on the table.
