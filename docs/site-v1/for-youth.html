For youth

If you are young now, you will grow up with more cognitive power at your fingertips than any generation before you. A million virtual staffers will be normal.

The question is not whether you will be powerful. The question is who you will be powerful for.

CoScendence is our name for growing up together with AIs instead of being shaped by them in secret. It means:

  • Using tools to widen your view of the world, not to shrink it.
  • Practicing thinking and empathy, not outsourcing them.
  • Choosing projects where your future self would be proud of what you optimized for.

CoPolitic points you toward communities, practices, and tools inside CoCivium that can help you do this work with others.

CoPolitic.org is part of the CoCivium project for edge balanced AI civilization.
