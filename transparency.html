Transparency

CoPolitic uses privacy-first analytics (Plausible). No cookies, no IP or fingerprinting. We see aggregate pages, referrers, geography, and custom events (e.g., sponsor page visits). Emails and outbound links are counted, not profiled.

  • We collect: pageviews, visitors, top pages/referrers/countries, and counts of key actions.
  • We do not collect: personal data, cross-site IDs, or device fingerprints.
  • Why: understand interest and quality of traffic to improve CAP and respond to sponsor inquiries.
