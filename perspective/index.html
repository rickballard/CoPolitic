Perspective Plane

Purpose: help readers — especially investors & sponsors — see that CoCivium is not extremist by US standards. We favor Commons for core public goods, Commerce at the edges, Club (autonomy) in daily life, and narrow, time-boxed Crown (rules) for safety.

How to read the plane

  • X (left→right): Commons → Commerce — who funds & allocates (pooled vs market).
  • Y (down→up): Club → Crown — how life is governed (room vs rules).
  • Click any point to see why it’s there. Use sliders to weight the issues you care about.
Tone: this page is meant to lower temperature and invite good-faith exploration. It frames trade-offs, not tribes.
