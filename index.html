TOS-AI

TOS-AI (Transition Office Steward – AI pivots) is a temporary executive steward who guides strategy, governance, and operating-model shifts during an organization’s AI transition. Learn more.

When to install a TOS-AI
Request a TOS-AI
  • AI strategy exists but governance, risk, and assurance are unclear.
  • Multiple workstreams need one accountable steward for the transition.
  • Org design, policies, or vendor choices will change within 1–2 quarters.
  • There’s a defined “exit criteria” to hand back steady-state ownership.
